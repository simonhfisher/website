A little about me...

Hey, I'm Simon, I quite like drinking, in particular drinking cocktails. During my time working in a bar
I learnt how to make a few interesting things, but mainly its just become a hobby the impetus of
which has always been 'can I make this at home instead of paying bar prices'. Most of the time the
answer is yes, however allot of drinks do require certain liquors or niche ingredients that maybe
aren't easy to find, have a limited shelf life or simply aren't going to be used for more than one
cocktail recipe. With that in mind I try to stick to fairly simple cocktails using lots of the same
ingredients throughout with only one or two items that can be made at home. I also try to keep
the bar equipment needed down to a minimum but for some reason I seem to accrue loads of that..
